## Brain imaging in Stroke and vascular neurology

#### CT Perfusion
1. [Great intro to CT perfusion imaging parameters] (https://youtu.be/_FtwOCRbUgA)
summary: important parameters: 
    - CBV: decreased CBV -> core infarct 
    - CBF: decreased CBF can be seen in both penumbra and infarct
    - TTP: increased in both infarct and penumbra
    - MTT: increased in both infarct and penumbra
    - infarct: decreased CBV, decreased CBF, increased TTP and MTT
    - penumbra: normal CBV, decreased or normal CBF, increased TTP and MTT (TTP more sensitive)
