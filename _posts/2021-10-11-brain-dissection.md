If you are one of those people who learn more from experiencing rather than reading dry facts in the books, brain dissection is definitely for you. There are multiple great 
resources available for free that go over brain anatomy using real brain and brain slices. Here is a few of these resources that I really enjoyed myself: 

- [University of Utah Brain Dissection](https://neurologicexam.med.utah.edu/adult/html/brain-dissections.html)
- [UBC Neuroanatomy](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL242bEng6nyIdshvi_ZUid_i3YctT75q9)
