As patients with movement disorders are mostly seen in subspecialty clinic, neurology residents do not get enough exposure to these disorders. Great on-line resources have been 
developed and are available mostly for free to facilitate learning about movement and its disorders. You can find a collection of these resources below: 

- [Yale Movement Disorders Modules](https://movementmodules.yale.edu/)
