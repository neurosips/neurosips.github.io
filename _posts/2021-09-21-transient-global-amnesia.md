> Case: A 70 year old woman with past medical history of hypertension, hyperlipidemia, left ICA fibromuscular dysplasia who presented
> with acute memory loss since 2 hours ago. Her daughter-in-law reports that she has been asking the same questions multiple times and it
> seems like she does not remember. Neurological exam notable for immediate recall 3/3 and delayed recall 0/3. Otherwise, neurological exam
> unremarkable.
